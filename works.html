LiftApp

An AI-Powered Gym Trainer powered by Google Gemini. LiftApp designed to provide tailored workout plans and diet plans for individuals based on their body and fitness goals. By combining the convenience of digital technology with the personalization of professional coaching, a simple yet convenient qr payment, the system aims to make effective fitness training more accessible, engaging, and sustainable for everyone.

Webazaar

Webazaar is an e-commerce website designed for online shoppers, allowing them to browse, select, and purchase a variety of clothes and accessories. Users can add items to their cart, view the total cost, and complete their purchase through a convenient checkout feature. The platform aims to provide a smooth and user-friendly shopping experience for fashion enthusiasts.

RWB

Run with Basty is a front-end design created using Figma, focused on showcasing running shoes. The application highlights different types of shoes, emphasizing their unique features and purposes. It also includes a blog section to provide tips, reviews, and insights for runners, combining both product presentation and informative content in a visually engaging interface.

Epbidatcom

Epbidatcom is a social networking website inspired by Friendster. It features user authentication with login functionality and allows users to post status updates that are visible to their friends. The platform supports live updates, ensuring that friends can see new posts in real-time, creating an interactive and connected social experience.

Queueing System

A simple queuing system that allows users to join a queue and receive real-time updates on their position. Administrators can view the list of users in the queue and manage who will be attended to next, ensuring an organized and efficient service experience.

To-Do List

to-do list application that allows users to organize and manage their daily tasks efficiently. Users can add, edit, and delete tasks, helping them keep track of their responsibilities and stay productive.
